What is an NGO?

A Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) is a non-profit, independent, and voluntary group that addresses social, cultural, environmental

Why NGOs matter

, or humanitarian challenges through programs, advocacy, and community partnerships.
  • Fill gaps in services where government or market solutions fall short.
  • Promote social equity and empower marginalized communities.
  • Act quickly during disasters with relief and rehabilitation.
  • Raise awareness on rights, health, climate, and equality.
  • Build capacity with training, research, and partnerships.
