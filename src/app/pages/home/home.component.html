About Us

Aashram is your bridge between compassion and action. We are an independent platform designed to bring together individuals, communities, and organizations who want to make a difference.

Across India, countless NGOs are working tirelessly in fields like child education, women empowerment, healthcare, environmental protection, animal welfare, rural development, and disaster relief. But for many donors, finding trustworthy NGOs and understanding their work can be a challenge. That’s where we step in.

Our Role: Aashram serves as a transparent connector — we don’t run NGOs ourselves, nor do we collect donations on our own. Instead, we make it easier for you to discover genuine organizations, learn about their impact, and donate directly to them through their official channels.

Our platform is built to save you time, provide verified information, and give you confidence that your contribution is going exactly where it’s needed. Whether you’re a first-time donor or someone looking to support multiple causes, Aashram makes your giving journey simple, safe, and meaningful.

Our Services

We provide a complete gateway for connecting donors with impactful causes:

  • ✔
    Verified NGO Directory: Access a curated list of NGOs across all major sectors in India.
  • ✔
    Cause-Based Navigation: Search NGOs based on focus areas like education, health, environment, disaster relief, and more.
  • ✔
    Direct Donation Links: Donate securely through the official NGO portals, without middlemen.

  • ✔
