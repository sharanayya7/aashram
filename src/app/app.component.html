Top Healthcare NGOs in India

Doctors For You

Doctors For You is a humanitarian NGO founded in 2007 that provides emergency medical care, disaster relief, and public health services across India, especially in underserved areas.
🌐 Website: www.doctorsforyou.org

Karuna Trust

Karuna Trust is a public health NGO founded in 1986 in Karnataka. It focuses on rural healthcare, managing primary health centers (PHCs) in underserved areas, and works on disease control, mental health, and community development.
🌐 Website: karunatrust.org

SNEHA

SNEHA (Society for Nutrition, Education and Health Action) is a Mumbai-based NGO founded in 1999. It works to improve maternal and child health, prevent gender-based violence, and promote nutrition and healthcare in urban slums.
🌐 Website: www.snehamumbai.org

The Banyan

The Banyan is a Chennai-based NGO founded in 1993 that provides mental health care and rehabilitation for the homeless and marginalized. It runs hospitals, community mental health programs, and inclusive housing solutions.
🌐 Website: www.thebanyan.org

Smile Foundation

Smile Foundation is an NGO in India (est. 2002) that provides healthcare, education, and support to underprivileged children and families.
🌐 Website: www.smilefoundationindia.org

Aravind Eye Care

Aravind Eye Care is a non-profit eye care system founded in 1976 in Madurai. It provides high-quality, affordable eye care, especially to the poor, and is one of the largest providers of eye surgeries globally.
🌐 Website: www.aravind.org

Operation ASHA

Operation ASHA is a healthcare NGO founded in 2006 that focuses on tuberculosis (TB) detection, treatment, and awareness among underserved populations using low-cost, technology-enabled solutions.
🌐 Website: www.opasha.org

SEWA

SEWA (Self Employed Women’s Association) is a women’s trade union founded in 1972 in Ahmedabad. It empowers poor, self-employed women by providing access to healthcare, livelihood, childcare, and social security.
🌐 Website: www.sewa.org

About Us

Aashram NGO is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting underprivileged communities. Our mission is to provide education, healthcare, and support to those in need, empowering individuals to build a better future. Through various initiatives and the support of our volunteers and donors, we strive to create lasting positive change.

Our Services

We offer a range of services including free education programs, healthcare camps, vocational training, and community support initiatives. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that help reaches those who need it most.
