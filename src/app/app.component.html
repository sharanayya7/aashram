About Us

Aashram NGO is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting underprivileged communities. Our mission is to provide education, healthcare, and support to those in need, empowering individuals to build a better future. Through various initiatives and the support of our volunteers and donors, we strive to create lasting positive change.

Our Services

We offer a range of services including free education programs, healthcare camps, vocational training, and community support initiatives. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that help reaches those who need it most.
